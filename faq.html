FAQ

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes. Send your job details and photos and we will provide a no-obligation quote.

How quickly can you respond in Southland?

We aim to respond promptly and confirm scheduling based on current workload and location.

What areas do you service?

Invercargill and wider Southland, including Winton, Gore, Riverton, and surrounding rural areas.

How deep do you grind stumps?

Depth depends on your planned use of the area. Let us know if you are replanting, paving, or laying lawn.

Can you work in tight-access spaces?

Yes, we handle many jobs with restricted access, fences, and nearby structures.

Do you remove mulch and debris?

Yes. We can remove green waste or leave mulch on-site by request.

Can you grind near driveways and buildings?

Yes, with careful planning and safe operating clearances.

Are you insured?

Yes, we operate with safety-first practices and appropriate job planning.

What affects the final quote?

Stump size, number of stumps, site access, root spread, and cleanup requirements.

Do you do commercial and rural work?

Yes, we work for homeowners, landlords, farms, builders, and commercial sites.

Can you help with storm-damaged trees?

Yes, we can help clear damaged trees and unsafe debris as scheduling allows.

How do I book?

Call us or submit the quote form with your suburb, photos, and preferred timing.
